Brainstorm

The theme for the 48 hours game jam were, "make me laugh". Our group brainstormed several ideas: dark humors, pure randomness and stupidity. Then we landed on the idea of making a platformer about city rats, which is inspired by a tiktok I brought up in conversation.

Process

We splitted up the work, and I was the programmer for all the gameplay. My partner took up the work for art of the game, and I helped with some of the workload with sketching up some ideas. I would say the part of the game that brought out a lot of laugh is the uncapped jump limits.
